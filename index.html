CHRIS EVANS

Artist

Christopher Robert Evans (born June 13, 1981) is an American actor, film producer, and director. He gained wider recognition for his portrayal of Steve Rogers / Captain America in several Marvel Cinematic Universe films. In 2010, Evans signed on for a multi-film deal with Marvel Studios, to portray Marvel Comics character Steve Rogers / Captain America. Evans initially turned down the part, but he consulted with Robert Downey Jr., who encouraged him to take the role. At Marvel's persistence, Evans accepted doing the part.

PROFILE

CAPTAIN AMERICA

Avengers

Captain America is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character wears a costume bearing an American flag motif, and he utilizes a nearly-indestructible shield that he throws as a projectile. On July 16, 2014, Marvel Comics announced that the mantle of Captain America would be passed on by Steve Rogers. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Steve Rogers was portrayed by Chris Evans.
